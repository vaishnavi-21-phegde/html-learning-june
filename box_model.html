A paragraph is a distinct unit of writing, typically composed of multiple sentences, that focuses on a single idea or topic. It serves to organize and structure written work, making it easier for readers to follow the author's train of thought. A well-structured paragrap usually includes a topic sentence, supporting sentences, and sometimes a concluding sentence. Key characteristics of a paragraph: Single Idea: Each paragraph should focus on one main idea or topic.
